Rui Patric Chowdhury- The head chef of this resturant is one of the top ranked chef in India got Master Chef Oriental Cuisine runner ups(2023) including Master Chef North Indian Cuisine winner(2020).

Welcome to "Book_o_Vibe," where culinary creativity meets literary inspiration. Our menu is a carefully crafted fusion of flavors that takes you on a gastronomic journey inspired by classic and contemporary literature. Start your culinary adventure with our "Fable Feast" appetizers, featuring whimsical bites like "Crispy Corn Fry" and "Classic Margarita Mocktail." Delve into our main courses with options like the "Hyderabadi Mutton Biriyani" or the "Kerala Mutton Ishtew." Indulge into spicy mouthwatering chinese recepies such as the "Chicken Schezwan Hakka Noodles" with "Baby Corn Mushroom Masala." Each dish is a carefully curated blend of ingredients, textures, and literary references, promising a dining experience that transcends the ordinary. At Book_o_Vibe, we invite you to savor not only the delectable flavors but also the literary inspirations that accompany each dish, creating a truly unique and immersive dining atmosphere.
